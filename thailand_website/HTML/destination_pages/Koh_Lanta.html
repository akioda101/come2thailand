Koh Lanta

Island Paradise Unveiled

About Koh Lanta

Koh Lanta is a tranquil island located off the Andaman Coast in Southern Thailand. Known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and laid-back atmosphere, it offers a perfect escape from the bustling tourist hotspots. The island is rich in cultural diversity, home to the Chao Leh (Sea Gypsies) and offering a mix of Thai, Chinese, and Muslim influences. Whether you're looking to relax on pristine beaches, explore lush jungles, or immerse yourself in local culture, Koh Lanta has something for everyone.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Relax on Long Beach and enjoy breathtaking sunsets.
  • Explore Mu Ko Lanta National Park with its lighthouse and hiking trails.
  • Visit the Sea Gypsy Village to experience local culture.
  • Kayak through the mangrove forests of Tung Yee Peng.
  • Discover the Khlong Chak Waterfall and nearby bat cave.
