About Chiang Mai

Nestled in the mountainous region of northern Thailand, Chiang Mai is a city rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Rose of the North," it offers a blend of ancient temples, bustling markets, and lush green landscapes. From exploring the Old City to trekking in the nearby mountains, Chiang Mai provides a unique and tranquil escape for travelers.
