Khao Yai

Thailand's Nature Retreat

About Khao Yai

Khao Yai, Thailand's oldest and third-largest national park, is a haven for nature lovers and adventurers. Just a few hours from Bangkok, it boasts lush forests, rolling hills, and abundant wildlife, including elephants, gibbons, and hornbills. The park is part of the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Dong Phayayen-Khao Yai Forest Complex. With scenic waterfalls, hiking trails, and picturesque vineyards, Khao Yai offers a refreshing escape from the city hustle.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Embark on wildlife safaris to spot elephants, deer, and exotic birds.
  • Visit Haew Suwat Waterfall, made famous by the movie "The Beach."
  • Hike through lush trails to experience the diverse flora and fauna.
  • Explore the European-inspired Palio Village for shopping and dining.
  • Enjoy wine tasting at local vineyards like PB Valley and GranMonte.
