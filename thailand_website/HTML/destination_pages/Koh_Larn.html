Koh Larn

The Coral Island Getaway

About Koh Larn

Koh Larn, also known as Coral Island, is a small island located just off the coast of Pattaya in the Gulf of Thailand. Renowned for its white sandy beaches, clear turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs, it's a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking a tropical escape. The island offers a range of activities from water sports to relaxing beachside cafes, making it an ideal spot for a day trip or a quiet getaway.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Relax on Tawaen Beach, the island's most popular beach with crystal-clear waters.
  • Go snorkeling or scuba diving to explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
  • Enjoy panoramic views from the hilltop viewpoint overlooking the island.
  • Visit Samae Beach for water sports like parasailing and jet skiing.
  • Experience local seafood cuisine at beachfront restaurants.
