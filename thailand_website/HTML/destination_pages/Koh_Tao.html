Koh Tao

The Diver's Paradise

About Koh Tao

Koh Tao, meaning "Turtle Island," is a small island located in the Gulf of Thailand. Famous for its stunning coral reefs and abundant marine life, it is one of the world's most popular destinations for scuba diving and snorkeling. Beyond the underwater attractions, Koh Tao offers beautiful beaches, hiking trails with breathtaking viewpoints, and a relaxed island atmosphere. Whether you're an avid diver or simply seeking a tropical paradise, Koh Tao is a must-visit destination.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Get PADI certified and explore the vibrant coral reefs.
  • Enjoy the sunset at Sairee Beach, the island's longest beach.
  • Hike to John-Suwan Viewpoint for panoramic views of the island.
  • Snorkel at Shark Bay to spot blacktip reef sharks and sea turtles.
  • Take a day trip to the nearby Koh Nang Yuan Island.
