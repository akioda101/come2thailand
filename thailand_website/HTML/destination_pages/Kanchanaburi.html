Kanchanaburi

Where History Meets Nature

About Kanchanaburi

Kanchanaburi, a tranquil town in western Thailand, is famed for its World War II history and breathtaking natural beauty. Home to the historic Bridge over the River Kwai and the Death Railway, the town also offers serene escapes to waterfalls, caves, and lush national parks. A visit to Kanchanaburi promises an emotional journey through history alongside unforgettable natural wonders.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Visit the iconic Bridge over the River Kwai.
  • Hike through Erawan National Park and marvel at Erawan Waterfalls.
  • Explore the Hellfire Pass Memorial Museum.
  • Take a ride on the historic Death Railway.
  • Discover the serene beauty of Lawa Cave and Sai Yok Noi Waterfall.
