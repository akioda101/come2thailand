About Pattaya

Pattaya is a vibrant coastal city located on the eastern Gulf of Thailand, known for its lively atmosphere, beautiful beaches, and diverse attractions. Once a quiet fishing village, it has transformed into a bustling resort destination offering everything from water sports and island hopping to cultural sites and nightlife. Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, Pattaya provides a dynamic blend of experiences for travelers of all ages.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Relax on Pattaya Beach and enjoy water activities like jet skiing and parasailing.
  • Explore the Sanctuary of Truth, a majestic wooden temple by the sea.
  • Visit Nong Nooch Tropical Garden for its stunning botanical displays and cultural shows.
  • Experience the vibrant nightlife on Walking Street.
  • Take a day trip to Coral Island (Koh Larn) for pristine beaches and snorkeling.
