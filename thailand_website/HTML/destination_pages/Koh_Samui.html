Koh Samui

The Tropical Paradise of Thailand

About Koh Samui

Koh Samui, Thailand's second-largest island, is renowned for its palm-fringed beaches, coconut groves, and dense, mountainous rainforest. The island combines luxury resorts with backpacker hostels, offering something for every traveler. From the bustling nightlife of Chaweng Beach to the tranquil retreats of Lamai and the cultural sites like the Big Buddha Temple, Koh Samui is a tropical paradise that caters to both relaxation and adventure.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Relax on Chaweng Beach, the island's most popular stretch of sand.
  • Visit the iconic Big Buddha Temple (Wat Phra Yai).
  • Explore Ang Thong Marine National Park with its stunning islands and lagoons.
  • Discover the natural beauty of Na Muang Waterfalls.
  • Stroll through Fisherman's Village in Bophut for shopping and dining.
