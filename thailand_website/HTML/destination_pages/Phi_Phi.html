Phi Phi Islands

The Jewel of the Andaman Sea

About Phi Phi Islands

The Phi Phi Islands are a stunning archipelago located in the Krabi Province of Southern Thailand. Famous for their dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, the islands offer a slice of paradise. Maya Bay, featured in the movie "The Beach," is one of the most iconic spots. With opportunities for snorkeling, diving, hiking, and experiencing lively beach parties, Phi Phi Islands are a must-visit destination for travelers seeking both adventure and relaxation.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Visit Maya Bay to experience its stunning beauty (check for seasonal closures).
  • Go snorkeling or scuba diving to explore vibrant coral reefs.
  • Hike to the Phi Phi Viewpoint for panoramic views of the islands.
  • Explore Viking Cave and learn about its ancient paintings.
  • Enjoy the nightlife at Tonsai Bay with beach bars and fire shows.
