About Krabi

Krabi is a coastal province in southern Thailand characterized by stunning limestone cliffs, lush mangrove forests, and over a hundred offshore islands. Known for its idyllic beaches, clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life, Krabi is a paradise for beach lovers and adventure seekers. From rock climbing at Railay Beach to exploring the Phi Phi Islands, Krabi offers a diverse range of activities that showcase the natural beauty of Thailand's Andaman Coast.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Rock climb the limestone cliffs at Railay Beach.
  • Take a boat tour to the famous Phi Phi Islands.
  • Swim in the crystal-clear waters of the Emerald Pool.
  • Climb to the summit of Tiger Cave Temple for panoramic views.
  • Snorkel or dive around Hong Island to explore vibrant coral reefs.
