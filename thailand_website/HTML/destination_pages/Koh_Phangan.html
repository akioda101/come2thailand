Koh Phangan

The Island of Full Moon Parties and Tranquil Beaches

About Koh Phangan

Koh Phangan is a lush tropical island located in the Gulf of Thailand, famous for its monthly Full Moon Party. Beyond the vibrant nightlife, the island offers serene beaches, dense jungles, and a rich cultural heritage. Visitors can enjoy a mix of relaxation and adventure, from yoga retreats and meditation centers to hiking trails and snorkeling spots. Koh Phangan's diverse attractions make it a unique destination for all types of travelers.

Top Rated Activities & Events

  • Experience the world-famous Full Moon Party on Haad Rin Beach.
  • Relax on the secluded sands of Bottle Beach.
  • Explore the island's interior by hiking to Phaeng Waterfall.
  • Visit Thong Sala Night Market for local food and crafts.
  • Join a yoga or meditation retreat in the tranquil north.
