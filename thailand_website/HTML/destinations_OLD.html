Where to go

Discover Thailand's top destinations. Each city offers unique experiences, Find out more by having a look below.

Central Thailand Why here Travel to/from
Bangkok
  • Take a traditional long-tail boat tour through the Thonburi canals
  • Explore the bustling Pak Khlong Talad Flower Market
  • Visit the unique art exhibits at Bangkok Art and Culture Centre
  • Experience a Muay Thai match at Rajadamnern Stadium
  • Indulge in a luxury dinner cruise along the Chao Phraya River
 30-45 minutes from Suvarnabhumi or Don Mueang Airports. Accessible by train, bus, taxi, or Airport Rail Link.
Pattaya
  • Watch the Kaan Show, a mix of live performance and cinematic effects
  • Visit the Pattaya Floating Market for unique crafts and food
  • Explore the underwater world at Pattaya's Underwater World Aquarium
  • Visit Mini Siam for miniature replicas of famous landmarks
  • Take a traditional Thai pottery workshop at a local studio
 150 km from Bangkok, about 2 hours by car or 2.5 hours by bus.
Khao Yai
  • Stay at themed resorts like Palio Village for a Tuscan vibe
  • Spot gibbons on a night safari in Khao Yai National Park
  • Pick fresh strawberries and produce at local farms
  • Visit the magical Primo Piazza with its Italian-style architecture
  • See rare hornbills in their natural habitat
 170 km northeast of Bangkok, around 3 hours by car or 4 hours by train to Pak Chong.
Ayutthaya
  • Enjoy a cycling tour through the ancient ruins
  • Learn traditional Thai crafting techniques at a local artisan workshop
  • Attend a traditional Thai puppet performance
  • Visit Bang Pa-In Royal Palace
  • Take a sunset boat cruise around Ayutthaya’s historic temples
 85 km north of Bangkok, 1.5 hours by car, bus, or train.
Kanchanaburi
  • Take a bamboo raft ride along the River Kwai
  • Explore the mesmerizing Lawa Cave
  • Visit a local Mon tribal village for cultural immersion
  • Tour the WWII-era Kanchanaburi Cemetery and museums
  • See the picturesque Sai Yok Noi waterfalls
 130 km west of Bangkok, about 2.5-3 hours by train, bus, or car.
Koh Larn Island
  • Explore the coral reefs with sea-walking tours
  • Try parasailing for breathtaking aerial views
  • Discover hidden beaches like Tien Beach
  • Watch the sunset at Samae Beach viewpoint
  • Enjoy the fresh seafood markets at Na Ban Pier
 Ferry from Pattaya takes 30-45 minutes. Pattaya is 2 hours from Bangkok by car or bus.
Hua Hin
  • Explore the whimsical Santorini Park
  • Take a horseback ride along Hua Hin Beach
  • Visit the royal Maruekhathaiyawan Palace
  • Hike to Pala-U Waterfall in Kaeng Krachan National Park
  • Tour local vineyards at Monsoon Valley
 200 km southwest of Bangkok, about 3 hours by car or 3.5 hours by train or bus.
South Thailand Why here Travel to/from
Koh Sok National Park
  • Explore caves like Nam Talu Cave
  • Spend a night at one of the eco-friendly resorts within the park
  • Walk through the oldest evergreen rainforest in the world
  • Enjoy stargazing in the pristine dark skies
  • Interact with local communities for a hands-on cooking experience
 110 km from Surat Thani, 2 hours by bus or 1.5 hours by car.
Koh Lanta Island
  • Join a mangrove exploration tour in Tung Yee Peng
  • Visit the Sea Gypsy Village for cultural insights
  • Take a cooking class to learn authentic southern Thai dishes
  • Hike to Khlong Chak Waterfall
  • Explore caves at Mai Kaew Cave
 Ferry from Krabi (1.5 hours) or Phuket (2.5-3 hours). Krabi is 1.5 hours by flight from Bangkok.
Koh Phangan Island
  • Take a herbal sauna experience at Wat Pho
  • Go to the Full Moon party - February 13th
  • Explore Than Sadet Waterfall National Park
  • Learn to make tie-dye at a local workshop
  • Visit the Chinese Temple (Guanyin) for panoramic views
 Ferry from Koh Samui (30-60 minutes) or Surat Thani (2.5-3 hours). Flights to Koh Samui from Bangkok take 1 hour.
Koh Samui Island
  • Take an eco-tour through Samui Elephant Sanctuary
  • Visit Secret Buddha Garden
  • Try paddleboarding at Bang Po Beach
  • Discover the unique mummified monk at Wat Khunaram
  • Explore traditional markets like Fisherman’s Village
 1 hour by flight from Bangkok or 1.5-2 hours by ferry from Surat Thani.
Krabi
  • Take a scenic kayaking trip in Ao Thalane Bay
  • Explore Khao Khanab Nam caves
  • Visit Wat Kaew Korawaram for unique architecture
  • Tour the Krabi Shell Cemetery
  • Enjoy a traditional long-tail boat ride through mangroves
 1.5 hours by flight from Bangkok or a 3-hour drive from Phuket.
Phi Phi Islands
  • Swim in the emerald-green Pileh Lagoon
  • Enjoy rock climbing adventures on Koh Bida
  • Discover Viking Cave with ancient wall paintings
  • Experience traditional fishing with local guides
  • Take a private sunrise boat tour to avoid crowds
 Ferry from Krabi (1.5-2 hours) or Phuket (2 hours).
Koh Tao Island
  • Take a sunset hike to Love Koh Tao viewpoint
  • Visit Ao Leuk Beach for its serene atmosphere
  • Explore coral nurseries at Hin Wong Bay
  • Learn free diving techniques at a local school
  • Experience marine conservation programs
 Ferry from Koh Samui (2 hours) or Surat Thani (3 hours).
Phuket
  • Visit the Phuket Elephant Sanctuary
  • Tour the Peranakan (Baba-Nyonya) Museum
  • Enjoy a traditional Thai rum-tasting tour at Chalong Bay
  • Explore the Sirinat National Park for untouched beaches
  • Experience traditional boat building at a local village
 1.5 hours by flight from Bangkok or 12 hours by bus.
