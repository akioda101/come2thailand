What to Do

Dive into Thailand's vibrant activities designed for young adventurers! Whether you're seeking thrill, culture, or relaxation, this list is packed with top recommendations to make your trip unforgettable.

Cooking Classes

There’s no better way to get acquainted with Thai culture than during a cooking class in Bangkok.
Master the art of some classic Thai dishes—including pad Thai noodles and red Thai curry—and overcome the language barrier during a market tour.
This experience proves ideal for food lovers and first-time visitors.

National Parks

Thailand is gifted with some of the oldest virgin woodlands in the world.

Cultural Tours

Explore ancient temples and bustling markets to immerse yourself in Thai culture.

Visit the Night Markets

Thailand is home to some of the most vibrant night-markets in the world.

Elephant Sanctuaries

Interact responsibly with rescued elephants in ethical sanctuaries.

(Please make sure to verify if the sanctuary is an ethical one)

Canoeing & Kayaking

Paddle through mangroves and sea caves in Phuket and Krabi.

Nightlife

Experience buzzing nightlife in Bangkok and Pattaya with clubs, bars, and rooftop lounges.

Floating Markets

Enjoy unique shopping experiences on boats at floating markets like Damnoen Saduak.

Muay Thai

Take a Muay Thai class or watch live fights for an adrenaline rush.

Island Hopping

Visit stunning islands like Koh Phi Phi, Koh Samui, and Koh Tao.

Canal-boat restaurants

Explore scenic coastlines and hidden beaches on a traditional longtail boat.

Hot Air Balloon Rides

Enjoy breathtaking sunrise views over Chiang Mai’s countryside.
