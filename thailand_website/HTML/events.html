Festivals and Events in February

Experience the vibrant culture of Thailand this February. From lively festivals to traditional ceremonies, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Chinese New Year

Celebrate the Lunar New Year in Chinatown, Bangkok, with vibrant parades, dragon dances, and delicious street food. This festival brings good fortune and prosperity to all.

Chiang Mai Flower Festival

Admire breathtaking floral displays, floats, and parades in the beautiful city of Chiang Mai. This festival highlights the region's rich horticultural heritage.

Full Moon Party

Join the world-famous beach party on Koh Phangan, where music, dancing, and fire shows light up the night under the full moon.

Makha Bucha Day

Honor Buddhist traditions with candlelit processions at temples across Thailand. This sacred day commemorates the teachings of the Buddha.
